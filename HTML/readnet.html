Networking Essentials Workshop

Date: December 15, 2024
Time: 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM
Venue: Kongu Engineering College, Erode

About the Workshop

The **Networking Essentials Workshop** is a hands-on session crafted for professionals and students who wish to excel in building and maintaining strong professional relationships. Whether you're attending conferences, engaging in one-on-one meetings, or exploring online networking platforms like LinkedIn, this workshop will equip you with essential tools and strategies to stand out and leave a lasting impression.

Key Takeaways

  • Learn to craft a compelling elevator pitch that captures attention.
  • Master the art of starting and sustaining meaningful conversations.
  • Develop effective follow-up techniques to maintain professional connections.
  • Understand the power of personal branding in networking.
  • Gain confidence to network effectively in virtual and in-person environments.

Who Should Attend?

This workshop is ideal for:

  • Students preparing to enter the workforce.
  • Professionals looking to expand their networks for career growth.
  • Entrepreneurs seeking connections to scale their businesses.
  • Anyone who feels shy or unsure about networking and wants to improve their skills.
